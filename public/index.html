Frequently asked questions

Does WedEazzy charge me anything?
No. Browsing, inquiring, and booking vendors via WedEazzy is 100% free for couples. We earn from optional featured listings paid by vendors.
Are these vendors verified?
Yes. Every listing is checked for active phone, working address, and a valid Google Maps presence. Ratings shown are pulled from public Google reviews where available.
How do I get a quote?
Open any vendor profile, fill the short inquiry form (or hit the WhatsApp button). The vendor receives your details on WhatsApp instantly. Most respond within an hour during business hours.
Can WedEazzy plan my full wedding?
We aren't planners — but we can shortlist trusted wedding planners on the platform and route your brief to them. WhatsApp our team and we'll help.
Do you cover destination weddings?
Yes — we have curated vendors across Goa (beach weddings), Udaipur (palace/lake), Jaipur (haveli), Jodhpur (fort) and Mumbai (5-stars + farmhouses).
I'm a vendor — how do I list?
Hit "List Your Business Free" or message +91 74989 87620. We onboard new vendors within 24 hours.
